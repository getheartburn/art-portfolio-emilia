El Coche Catrin loosely translates to The Fancy Car. My fancy car had metal flowers screwed onto the frame and talavera tiles glued to the sides. The entire body was hand painted using latex paint.  The hood was the main centerpiece. It had a portrait of a Day of the Dead style skull.
